Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

TL;DR: Your financial data stays on your device. We don't upload, sell, or share it. AI categorization runs locally. DPDP Act 2023 compliant.

1. Introduction

SpendWise ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

2. Information We Collect

A. SMS and Notification Data

We access your device's notifications solely for the purpose of:

  • Detecting financial transactions (debits/credits)
  • Extracting transaction details (Amount, Merchant, Date)
  • Categorizing your expenses

We DO NOT:

  • Read your personal SMS messages (OTP, personal chats)
  • Read notifications from non-financial apps (WhatsApp, Instagram, etc.)
  • Upload your raw notification content to any cloud server

All processing of notification text happens locally on your device.

B. User Account Data (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google:

  • We collect your Email Address and Name for authentication
  • This data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS)

C. Financial Data

  • Your transaction history is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database
  • If you enable "Cloud Backup" (future feature), encrypted backups may be stored on your Google Drive or our secure servers

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Expense Tracking: To provide you with dashboards and insights
  • AI Categorization: We use a local AI model (Gemma) to categorize transactions. No data leaves your device for this.
  • Cloud AI (Optional): If you enable Gemini Cloud features, anonymized transaction text (merchant name) may be sent to Google's Gemini API for advanced insights. This is optional and disabled by default.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data.

5. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard encryption (SQLCipher) for your local database
  • Network communication uses SSL/TLS

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all your data permanently via Settings > Privacy > Delete My Data
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Contact our Grievance Officer at indraprakashgottipati61@gmail.com
